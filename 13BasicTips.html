Before a disaster strikes, it's crucial to be prepared. Here are some essential safety tips:
  • Secure your home: Fix shelves to walls, remove heavy objects from high places, and ensure electrical fixtures are secure.
  • Emergency Supplies: Keep a kit with water,food,first-aid items,medicine,tools,a flashlight,batteries,blanket,and a fire-extinguisher.
  • Know your exits:Identify safe places in your home and ensure clear exit routes.
  • Gas & Electricity Safety:Know the location of master switches and shut-off valves to privient fires or leaks.
  • Stay & Informed:Keep a battery-powered radio to recieve emergency updates.
